Where2Play

See where everyone's playing — before you go.

Install on iPhone Install on Android
After you tap your button:
  1. Install, open the app, and sign up (Google, Apple, or email).
  2. Choose your sport, then set your level and nickname.
  3. When it asks for your location, choose “Always Allow” — that's what shows you at the courts.
  4. Tap Allow for notifications so you know when players show up.
Android: if a message says “install unknown apps,” tap Allow → Install.
